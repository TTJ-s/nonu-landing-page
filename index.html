Who are We

Live For Cause is a non-profit organization working for a social cause.We work with the motto of helping the most vulnerable sections of society to live a life with dignity and equal rights. We are also active in spreading social awareness, running campaigns, and standing for a cause.

What is

The LiveForCause Foundation is launching Project NONU (Nurturing Openness and Non-judgmental Understanding) to provide mental health support for those in need. Our aim is to establish a safe and supportive environment where individuals can receive assistance in attaining sound mental health. This will be achieved through a combination of online and offline activities, such as mental health screenings, therapy sessions under the supervision of clinical psychologists, mindfulness workshops, mental health fairs, and awareness campaigns. We are striving to create a safe space where people can be there for each other. Additionally, we plan to offer basic mental health training and celebrate important mental health days to promote responsibility and understanding.

Our
Goals

Create a safe and supportive space for individuals to share their mental health experiences, challenges, and triumphs.

Foster a sense of community and belonging among group members.

Raise awareness about the impact of mental health on individuals and society.

Provide training and resources to volunteers to equip them with the skills needed to provide effective support and advocacy.

Encourage self-care practices and coping strategies for managing mental health.

Our
Mission

At LiveForCause, our mission is to help the most vulnerable sections of society live a life with dignity and equal rights. Project NONU is one of the many initiatives we have launched to support our mission. We believe that mental health is an essential aspect of overall well-being, and we strive to make it accessible to all
