Delete Your Account

We respect your privacy. You can delete your Project Nonu account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion:
  1. Log in to your Project Nonu account.
  2. Go to Settings  Profile  Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your request.

If you cant access your account, email us at support@projectnonu.com with the subject Delete My Account and your registered email address.

What happens next:
  • Your personal data (name, email, preferences, uploads) will be permanently deleted within 7 days.
  • Some data (like transaction records) may be retained for up to 30 days as required by law.
  • Once deletion is complete, your account cannot be recovered.
